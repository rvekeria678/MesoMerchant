Our Goal

The purpose of this game is to enrich the education of students learning about the Mesopotamian era. In addition, it aims to fufill project expectations set by the college elective, Software Engineering I, offered by the University of Massachussets, Lowell. Our team was given the challenge of creating a game that could be deployed in an educational enviroment, enriching student engagement.

The Process

Our team followed philosphies of Agile Developement for the creation of MesoMerchant. The project had to have been completed within two months with the ability to showcase a demo at the end of the fall 2021 semeseter.
